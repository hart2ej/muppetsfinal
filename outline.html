Scene Outline

The formatted outline can be found here.

This scene depicts a bit of in media res for a typical dress rehearsal for Kermit's play, Jane Eyre: a play with music In the scene, Jane and Rochester are getting ready to be married. They are at the chapel, and the priest asks if anyone objects to the marriage. As in the novel, Jane does not suspect that anything could go awry, but it does. Briggs and Mason show up to stop the wedding in its tracks. As is revealed in the scene, Rochester already has an alive wife!

Jane Eyre meets the Muppets

This website will be a proof of concept for a Muppets adaptation of Charlotte Bronte's Jane Eyre, the 1847 fictional autobiography.

This project is part of my final for a Jane Eyre adaptations class. The final project is to adapt Jane Eyre (or one of its own many adaptations) into an adaptative work of our own. So I chose to use the Muppets as an avenue for adaptation. This project has been challenging in some interesting ways.
This website will take you through my rationale for why the Muppets would make a good Jane Eyre adaptation, as well as a cast list for my adaptation.
Much like how Jane Eyre is "not a typical autobiography," my adaptation will not be a typcial adapation (Bronte 99). My vision for it is a play written by Kermit, performed by both human and Muppet actors. The final product will be comprised of scenes both from Kermit's adaptation of Jane Eyre as well as "real world" scenes of the Muppets putting the play on.
